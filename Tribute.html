Jordan Belfort

The Real Wolf of Wall Street

The Rise and Fall

Jordan Belfort is an American author, motivational speaker, and former stockbroker. In the 1990s, he founded Stratton Oakmont, a brokerage house that participated in penny stock scams and stock market manipulation.

His life story became globally famous through his memoir, The Wolf of Wall Street, which was later adapted into a major motion picture directed by Martin Scorsese and starring Leonardo DiCaprio.

Career & Legacy

  • Stratton Oakmont: Built one of the largest private brokerage firms in the US during the 90s.
  • The Straight Line System: Developed a world-renowned sales training methodology used by corporations today.
  • Author: Penned two best-selling memoirs and several books on the art of persuasion and sales.
  • Motivational Speaking: Now travels the world teaching ethics in business and sales psychology.
