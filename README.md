The Wolf of Wall Street 

 During my Oasis Infobyte internship, I developed a high-energy tribute page dedicated to Jordan Belfort. This project allowed me to experiment with a "corporate luxury" design style while documenting his career, sales mastery, and public speaking evolution.
​Key Features:
​Bold UI Design: Utilizes a dark-mode, high-contrast aesthetic to match the finance industry vibe.
​Sales-Focused Layout: Highlighted the "Straight Line" persuasion system through clean, card-based components.
​Optimized Responsiveness: Engineered to maintain visual impact on everything from mobile phones to 4K monitors.
